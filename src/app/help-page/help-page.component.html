Course List Help

This page gives help on how to use the course list.

Searching for courses

Type in the course designators seperated by commas to search for something. Press "ENTER" or click "Click me!" to search. You may only search up the course designators: searching MAT137 won't return anything.

Course Card Contents

  • Click on the top half of a card (the course code) to expand the course description.
  • Click on the bottom of a card to open up the course lecture sections.

The color on the right side of the course cards represents the breath requirements it fulfills. Specifically:

  • Creative and cultural representations (1)

  • Thought, belief, and behavior (2)

  • Society and its institutions (3)

  • Living things and their environment (4)

  • The physical and mathematical universes (5)

  • None

The color on the bottom left of each course header represents the terms it is offered in.

  • The course is offered in the fall.
  • The course is offered in the winter.
  • The course is offered year-wide.

The color coding will be identical when you open up the card that shows the course's lecture sessions.
